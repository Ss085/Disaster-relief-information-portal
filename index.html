The Disaster Preparedness and Relief Information Portal is a comprehensive online resource designed to help individuals and communities prepare for, respond to, and recover from natural disasters. It provides essential information on different types of disasters, step-by-step preparedness plans, emergency contact details, and nearby shelter locations. The portal also includes downloadable guides, first-aid checklists, and links for disaster relief donations. Accessible and user-friendly, this platform aims to enhance public safety and community resilience during emergencies.

2023 Flash Floods in Sikkim Preparedness

  • Preparedness Plan:
    • Effected Area: Teesta Dam, Chungthang
    • Relocate to higher ground.
    • Avoid crossing flooded areas.
    • Emergency kit:
      • Water (at least 3 days' supply)
      • Non-perishable food items
      • First-aid kit
  • Emergency Contacts:
    • Sikkim Police: 03592-203478
    • Emergency Response Support Team: 112
    • Nearest Shelter: Mountain Rd, Sikkim
  • Resources:
    • First-aid checklists:
      • Bandages
      • Antiseptic wipes
      • Adhesive tape

2023 Landslides in Himachal Pradesh Preparedness

  • Preparedness Plan:
    • Effected Areas: Himalayan Expressway, sections of Kalka-Shimla Railway.
    • Evacuate when alerts are issued.
    • Avoid hilly or loose soil areas.
    • Emergency kit:
      • Water
      • Food (dry items)
      • Torches and batteries
  • Emergency Contacts:
    • Himachal Pradesh Police: 0177-2621711
    • Disaster Helpline: 0177-2929688
    • Nearest Shelter: 456 Hilltop Ave, Himachal Pradesh
  • Resources:
    • First-aid checklists:
      • Sanitation supplies
      • Bandages
      • Paracetamol or Dolo-650 tablets

2024 Cyclone Fengal Preparedness

  • Preparedness Plan:
    • Effected Areas: Coastal Bengal, Sundarbans.
    • Relocate to cyclone shelters or higher ground.
    • Secure loose objects in your surroundings.
    • Emergency kit:
      • Drinking water
      • Ready-to-eat food
      • First-aid kit
  • Emergency Contacts:
    • Bengal Disaster Management Authority: 033-22143526
    • Emergency Response Team: 112
    • Nearest Shelter: Cyclone Shelter, Sundarbans Area.
  • Resources:
    • First-aid checklists:
      • Antiseptic cream
      • Sterile gauze pads
      • Ibuprofen tablets

2024 Vijayawada Floods Preparedness

  • Preparedness Plan:
    • Effected Areas: Prakasam Barrage, Krishna River Basin.
    • Move to higher ground or flood shelters.
    • Avoid waterlogged roads and bridges.
    • Emergency kit:
      • Portable chargers
      • Non-perishable food items
      • Life jackets (if possible)
  • Emergency Contacts:
    • Andhra Pradesh Police: 0866-2451074
    • Emergency Response Team: 112
    • Nearest Shelter: Flood Shelter, Bhavanipuram, Vijayawada.
  • Resources:
    • First-aid checklists:
      • Thermal blankets
      • Disinfectant wipes
      • Adhesive bandages
